Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 15, 2025

At Trekka, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our transportation booking platform.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

Personal Information

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Your name, email address, and phone number
  • Payment details and billing information
  • Travel preferences and booking history
  • Account credentials and profile details
  • Communication records with our support team

Automatically Collected Information

When you use our platform, we also collect:

  • Device details (IP address, browser type, operating system)
  • Usage data (pages viewed, features used, time spent)
  • Location data (only with your permission)
  • Cookies and other tracking technologies

2. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

We use your information to:

  • Process bookings and manage transportation services
  • Send booking confirmations and trip updates
  • Process payments and prevent fraudulent activity
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Improve our platform and add new features
  • Send marketing messages or promotions (with your consent)
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations
  • Analyze usage trends and enhance user experience

3. INFORMATION SHARING

We may share your information with:

TRANSPORT COMPANIES

We share ony what's necessary [your name, contact details, and trip information] booking information with transport companies to complete your booking.

SERVICE PROVIDERS

We work with trusted third-parties for payments, emails, analytics, and customer support. They only use your data to perform their asigned services.

LEGAL AND SAFETY REQUIREMENTS

We may disclose information if required by law or to protect Trekka, our user, or the public.

BUSINESS TRANSFERS

If Trekka merges, is acquired, or sells assets, your information may transfer to the new entity.

4. DATA SECURITY

We protect your data using industry-standard security measure such as:

  • Data encryption (in transit and at rest)
  • Secure, PCI-compliant payment processing
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability tests
  • Restricted data access and user authentication
  • Staff training on data protection

While we take evry precaution, no online system is completely secure. We encourage you to keep your login informationsafe.

5. YOUR RIGHTS AND CHOICES

You have control over your data

Access and Download:

View and download your data in your account settings.

Edit and Update:

Update your information anytime from your dashboard.

Delete Account:

Request data deletion by contacting support. Some information may be retained as required by law.

Marketing preferences:

Unsubscribe from marketing emails or adjust your preferences in your account.

Cookies:

Manage Cookies through your browser settings or see our Cookies Policy page for more information.

6. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Trekka does not serve individuals under age 18. If we learn that a child's information has been collected, we will delete it immediately.

7. INTERNATIONAL DATA TRANSFERS

Your informationmay be stored or processed in countries outside your residence. We apply appropriate safeguards to protect your data wherever it's processed.

8. THIRD-PARTY LINKS

Our platform may include links to external websites. Trekka is not responsible for their privacy practices. Please review teir privacy policies before sharing personal information.

9. DATA RETENTION

We retain your information only as long as needed to:

  • Provide services and process bookings
  • Meet legal and financial obligations
  • Resolve disputes and enforce our terms
  • Prevent fraud and improve our services

Afterward, we delete or anonymize your data securely..

10. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any major changes will appear here with an updated "Last updated" date. Please check this page periodically for updates.

11. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about your privacy, contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@trekka.com

  • Address: 123 East Legon, Victoria Island, Lagos, Nigeria

Your trust matters to us. We are committed to keeping your data safe and your experience secure.
