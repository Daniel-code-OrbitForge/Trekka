REFUND POLICY

Last Updated: November 15, 2025

At Trekka, we understand that travel plans change. This Refund Policy explains how cancellations and refunds work, ensuring a fair process for both travelers and transport companies.

1. GENERAL REFUND POLICY

Refunds depend on when you cancel your booking in relation to your scheduled departure.

Cancellation Time Refund Amount Processing Fee
48+ hours before departure 90% refund 10% fee
24-48 hours before departure 50% refund 50% fee
Less than 24 hours before No refund 100% forfeited
No-show (missed departure) No refund 100% forfeited

2. HOW TO REQUEST A REFUND

To cancel your booking and request a refund:

  1. Log in to your Trekka account
  2. Go to "My Bookings."
  3. Select the trip you want to cancel
  4. Click "Cancel Booking" and confirm

Your refund will be processed automatically based on the timeline above.

You can also contact our support team at support@trekka.com or call +234 800 000 0000 for assistance.

3. REFUND PROCESSING TIME

After your cancellation is confirmed, refunds are processed as follows:

  • Card Payments: 5 to 7 business
  • Bank Transfers: 3 to 5 business days
  • Mobile Money: 1 to 3 business days

Wallet Credit: Instant (can be used for future bookings)

Processing times may vary depending on your bank or payment provider.

FULL REFUND ELIGIBILITY

You are eligible for a full refund in the following cases:

  • The transport company cancels your trip.
  • Your trip is delayed by more than 3 hours without prior notice.
  • The assigned vehicle is unavailable and no suitable replacement is provided.
  • There are verified safety issues with the vehicle or driver.
  • You experience a medical emergency (with proof).
  • Travel is disrupted by natural disasters or extreme weather condition.

NO REFUND SITUATIONS

Refunds are not provided in the following situations:

  • Personal change of plans without timely cancellation.
  • Late arrival at the pickup location.
  • Incorrect contact or booking details.
  • Minor delays under 30 minutes.
  • Complaints made after trip completion without prior notice.

5. PARTIAL REFUNDS

We may issue partial refunds at our discretions in cases such as:

  • Service quality below expected standards.
  • Minor vehicle issues affecting comfort but not safety.
  • Delays of 1 to 3 hours
  • Overbooking resolved with acceptable alternatives.

Partial refund amounts are determined individually after review.


6. REFUND TO ORIGINAL PAYMENT METHOD

Refunds are always issued to the original payment method used during booking.

If that payment method is unavailable, please contact our support team to arrange an alternative.


7. BOOKING MODIFICATIONS

Instead of canceling, you can modify your booking:

  • Date or time changes: Free if made 48+ hours before departure.
  • Route Changes: Allowed based on availability and price difference.
  • Passenger name changes: Allowed up to 24 hours before departure.

Modification fees are generally lower than cancellation fees.


8. GROUP BOOKINGS

For group bookings (5 or more passengers):

  • Partial cancellations may be allowed.
  • Refund depend on how many passengers cancel.
  • Contact support for specific terms.
  • Charter services may have unique refund rules.

9. PROMOTIONAL AND DISCOUNTED BOOKINGS

For bookings made with discounts or promo codes:

  • Some may be non-refundable.
  • Refund are based on discounted price paid.
  • Promo codes cannot be reused after cancellation.
  • Please check promotion terms before booking.

10. DISPUTE RESOLUTION

If you disagree with a refund decision:

  1. Contact support@trekka.com with your booking reference.
  2. Provide any supporting documents (e.g; receipts, photos, or medical proof).
  3. Our team will review your request within 3-5 business days.
  4. If unresolved, you may escalate to our management.

11. TRANSPORT COMPANY POLICIES

Some transport companies may have additional refund policies. These will be communicated during booking and will apply if they offer better terms for you.

12. FORCE MAJEURE

In the event of uncontrollable circumstances (e.g; war, pandemic, or government restrictions), special refund or rescheduling rules may apply. We will notify affected travelers by email and update our website accordingly.


13. CONTACT US

For refund inquiries or assistance:

  • Email: refunds@trekka.com
  • Phone: +234 800 000 0000 (24/7)
  • Support Portal: Submit a request

Please keep your booking reference handy when contacting us.

Quick Tips

  • Cancel 48+ hours early for best refund
  • Consider modifying instead of cancelling
  • Check company-specific policies
  • Keep your booking reference handy

Your satisfaction matters to us. While we hope your journey goes as planned, we're committed to making refunds fast, fair and transparent.
