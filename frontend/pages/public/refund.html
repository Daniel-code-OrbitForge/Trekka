Refund Policy

Last Updated: November 15, 2025

At Trekka, we understand that plans change. This Refund Policy explains our cancellation and refund procedures to ensure fair treatment for both travelers and transport companies.

1. General Refund Policy

Refunds are processed based on when you cancel your booking relative to the scheduled departure time. The following refund schedule applies to most bookings:

Cancellation Time Refund Amount Processing Fee
48+ hours before departure 90% refund 10% processing fee
24-48 hours before departure 50% refund 50% processing fee
Less than 24 hours before No refund 100% forfeited
No-show (missed departure) No refund 100% forfeited

2. How to Request a Refund

To cancel your booking and request a refund:

  1. Log into your Trekka account
  2. Navigate to "My Bookings"
  3. Select the booking you wish to cancel
  4. Click "Cancel Booking" and confirm
  5. Your refund will be processed automatically based on the timeline above

Alternatively, you can contact our support team at support@trekka.com or call +234 800 000 0000 for assistance.

3. Refund Processing Time

Once your cancellation is confirmed, refunds are processed as follows:

  • Card Payments: 5-7 business days to appear in your account
  • Bank Transfers: 3-5 business days
  • Mobile Money: 1-3 business days
  • Wallet Credit: Instant (can be used for future bookings)

Processing times may vary depending on your bank or payment provider.

4. Special Circumstances

Full Refund Eligibility

You may be eligible for a full refund in the following circumstances:

  • Transport Company Cancellation: If the transport company cancels your trip for any reason
  • Significant Delays: If departure is delayed by more than 3 hours without prior notice
  • Vehicle Issues: If the assigned vehicle is unavailable and no suitable alternative is provided
  • Safety Concerns: If you identify legitimate safety issues with the vehicle or driver
  • Medical Emergencies: With valid medical documentation
  • Natural Disasters: If travel becomes impossible due to natural disasters or extreme weather

No Refund Situations

Refunds are not provided in the following cases:

  • Personal change of plans without prior cancellation
  • Failure to arrive at the pickup location on time
  • Providing incorrect contact information
  • Minor delays under 30 minutes
  • Complaints made after trip completion without prior notification

5. Partial Refunds

In certain situations, partial refunds may be issued at our discretion:

  • Service did not meet advertised standards
  • Minor vehicle issues that affected comfort but not safety
  • Delays between 1-3 hours
  • Overbooking situations resolved with acceptable alternatives

Partial refund amounts are determined on a case-by-case basis.

6. Refund to Original Payment Method

Refunds are always issued to the original payment method used for booking. We cannot process refunds to different accounts or payment methods for security reasons.

If the original payment method is no longer available, please contact our support team for alternative arrangements.

7. Booking Modifications vs. Cancellations

Consider modifying your booking instead of cancelling:

  • Date/time changes may be free if done 48+ hours in advance
  • Route modifications subject to availability and price difference
  • Passenger name changes allowed up to 24 hours before departure

Modification fees are generally lower than cancellation fees.

8. Group Bookings

For group bookings (5+ passengers):

  • Partial cancellations may be permitted
  • Refund percentage based on number of passengers cancelled
  • Contact support for group booking modifications
  • Special terms may apply for charter services

9. Promotional and Discounted Bookings

Bookings made with promotional codes or discounts may have different refund terms:

  • Some promotional bookings may be non-refundable
  • Refund amounts calculated based on discounted price paid
  • Promotional codes cannot be reused after cancellation
  • Check specific promotion terms at time of booking

10. Dispute Resolution

If you disagree with a refund decision:

  1. Contact our support team with your booking reference and details
  2. Provide any supporting documentation (photos, receipts, medical certificates)
  3. Our team will review your case within 3-5 business days
  4. If still unresolved, you may escalate to our management team

11. Transport Company-Specific Policies

Some transport companies may have additional refund policies. These will be clearly displayed at the time of booking and take precedence over this general policy when more favorable to the customer.

12. Force Majeure

In cases of force majeure (war, pandemic, government restrictions), special refund or rescheduling policies may apply. We will communicate any such policies via email and on our website.

13. Contact Us

For refund inquiries or assistance:

  • Email: refunds@trekka.com
  • Phone: +234 800 000 0000 (24/7)
  • Support Portal: Submit a request

Please have your booking reference number ready when contacting us.

Your satisfaction matters. While we hope you never need to cancel, we're committed to making the process as fair and transparent as possible.
