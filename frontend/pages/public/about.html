About Trekka

Transforming the way people travel across cities


OUR MISSION

To lead Africa's transportation system by providing reliable, safe, and affordable travel through innovative technology and a customer-focused approach.

Mission Visual

CORE VALUES

These principles guide everything we do;

Safety First

We prioritize the safety and security of every traveler. All our partner companies and drivers undergo thorough verification and regular inspections.

Customer-Centric

Our travelers are at the heart of everything we do. We continuously improve our services based on your feedback and needs.

Integrity

We operate with honesty and transparency. No hidden fees, no surprises; just clear, fair pricing and reliable service.

Innovation

We embrace technology to make transportation booking easier, faster, and more convenient for everyone.

Community

We're building a community that includes travelers, transport companies, and drivers; all working together for better journeys.

Sustainability

We're committed to promoting eco-friendly transportation options and reducing our environmental impact.


Our Vision for the Future

To become the most trusted transportation platform across Africa and beyond.

Expanding to 50+ cities & countries within the next 5 years

Partnering with 2,000+ verified transport companies

Facilitating 10 million+ safe journeys annually

Introducing electric and hybrid vehicle options
