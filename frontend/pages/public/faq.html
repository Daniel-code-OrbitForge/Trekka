Booking Questions

Booking on Trekka is simple:

  1. Visit our website or app and click "Book a Ride"
  2. Enter your pickup location, destination, and travel date
  3. Choose your preferred vehicle type
  4. Browse available transport companies and select one
  5. Review the details and confirm your booking
  6. Make payment to secure your reservation

You'll receive instant confirmation via email and SMS with your booking reference.

You can book trips up to 90 days in advance. We recommend booking at least 24 hours ahead for popular routes to ensure availability, especially during peak travel seasons and holidays.

Yes, you can modify your booking up to 24 hours before departure, subject to availability. Changes may incur a modification fee depending on the transport company's policy. To modify, log into your account and select "Manage Booking" or contact our support team.

If you miss your scheduled trip without prior notice, your booking will be marked as a no-show and standard cancellation policies apply. We recommend arriving at least 15 minutes before departure. If you're running late, contact the transport company immediately using the details in your booking confirmation.

Absolutely! When booking, simply enter the traveler's details instead of your own. Make sure to provide their correct contact information so they receive booking confirmations and updates. The person making the payment doesn't have to be the passenger.

Payment & Pricing

We accept multiple secure payment methods:

  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, Mastercard, Verve)
  • Bank Transfers
  • Mobile Money
  • USSD Banking
  • Digital Wallets (Paystack, Flutterwave)

All payments are processed through secure, PCI-compliant payment gateways.

No! We believe in complete transparency. The price you see at checkout is the final price you pay. This includes the base fare, applicable taxes, and our small service fee. Any additional charges (like extra luggage or special requests) will be clearly displayed before you confirm your booking.

Payment is processed immediately upon booking confirmation. This secures your reservation with the transport company. You'll receive a payment receipt via email instantly.

Our refund policy varies by transport company but generally:

  • Cancellations 48+ hours before: 90% refund
  • Cancellations 24-48 hours before: 50% refund
  • Cancellations less than 24 hours: No refund

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days. See our Refund Policy for complete details.

Yes! We use industry-leading security measures. All payment information is encrypted using SSL technology and processed through PCI DSS-compliant payment gateways (Paystack and Flutterwave). We never store your complete card details on our servers.

For Transport Companies

We'd love to have you! Visit our Partnership page and fill out the application form. Our team will review your application and reach out within 48 hours. You'll need to provide:

  • Company registration documents
  • Vehicle documentation and insurance
  • Driver credentials and licenses
  • Bank account information for payouts

To ensure quality and safety, we require:

  • Registered transport company with valid documentation
  • Vehicles manufactured within the last 10 years
  • Valid vehicle insurance and roadworthiness certificates
  • Professional drivers with clean records
  • Commitment to our safety and service standards

We process payouts weekly. After deducting our service commission (typically 10-15%), funds are transferred directly to your registered bank account. You can track all transactions and earnings in real-time through your company dashboard.

Yes! You have full control over your pricing. We provide market insights and pricing recommendations, but the final decision is yours. Competitive pricing and quality service help attract more bookings.

Safety & Security

Safety is our top priority. Every driver and vehicle goes through rigorous verification:

  • Background checks on all drivers
  • Verification of driver licenses and credentials
  • Vehicle inspection and roadworthiness certification
  • Insurance verification
  • Regular safety audits and reviews

Your safety is paramount. In case of emergency:

  1. Call our 24/7 emergency hotline: +234 800 000 0000
  2. Use the SOS button in your booking confirmation email
  3. Contact local emergency services if needed (police, medical)

We track all trips and can provide immediate assistance.

Yes! Once your trip begins, you can track your journey in real-time through your Trekka account or the tracking link sent via SMS. Share this link with family or friends for added peace of mind.

We take all concerns seriously. You can report issues through:

  • The "Report Issue" button in your booking
  • Our customer support: support@trekka.com
  • Phone: +234 800 000 0000
  • Contact form on our website

We investigate all reports and take appropriate action promptly.
